Join Unity SMP

Becoming part of Unity SMP is simple! Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: Join Our Discord

Our Discord server is the heart of our community. This is where you'll find announcements, connect with other players, and submit your application.

Join Discord Server

Step 2: Read the Rules

Before applying, make sure you've read and understood our community guidelines. These rules help maintain a positive environment for all players.

Step 3: Submit Your Application

Fill out the application form in our Discord server. Tell us about yourself, your Minecraft experience, and why you'd like to join Unity SMP. Our team reviews applications regularly.

Step 4: Get Whitelisted

Once your application is approved, you'll receive the server IP and be added to the whitelist. You'll also get access to exclusive Discord channels.

Step 5: Start Playing!

Launch Minecraft, connect to the server, and begin your journey. Don't hesitate to ask questions or request help from our friendly community members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Unity SMP. Can't find what you're looking for? Join our Discord and ask!

Getting Started

How do I join Unity SMP?

To join Unity SMP, you'll need to apply through our Discord server. Once your application is reviewed and approved, you'll be whitelisted and can start playing. Check out our Join page for detailed steps.

What version of Minecraft do you use?

We currently run on the latest stable version of Minecraft Java Edition. Check our Discord announcements for the exact version number and any modpack requirements.

Is the server free to play?

Yes! Unity SMP is completely free to play. We're a community-driven server that relies on the passion and dedication of our players.

What are the age requirements?

We welcome players of all ages, but we recommend players be at least 13 years old to fully enjoy the community experience and follow our guidelines. Players under 13 should have parental permission.

Technical Support

What devices and platforms are supported?

Unity SMP runs on Minecraft Java Edition for Windows, Mac, and Linux. We cannot provide support for Bedrock Edition at this time.

The server seems laggy. Is this normal?

Some lag can occur during peak hours or major events. If you're experiencing consistent lag, try restarting your client first. If the problem persists, report it in our Discord support channel.

Can I use OptiFine or other performance mods?

Yes! OptiFine and most performance-enhancing mods are allowed and encouraged. Just make sure they don't give you unfair advantages over other players. All mods must comply with our server rules.

My game crashed. How do I get help?

Game crashes happen! Please share your crash log in our Discord support channel. Our technical team will do their best to help you identify and fix the issue.

Gameplay & Features

Are there any custom features or plugins?

Yes! We have several quality-of-life plugins and custom features to enhance your gameplay experience. These include player shops, land claiming, and community events. Join the server to discover them all!

Can I create shops or trade with other players?

Absolutely! We encourage player-to-player trading and have designated shopping districts where you can set up your own shops. Check in-game for more details.

How do I protect my builds?

We have land claiming features available to protect your builds from griefing. Instructions on how to claim land are available in-game and on our Discord.

Are there community projects I can join?

Yes! We regularly organize community builds, events, and collaborative projects. Keep an eye on our Discord announcements to participate in upcoming projects.

Rules & Moderation

What happens if I break the rules?

Rule violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server, depending on severity. Our moderation team handles each case fairly and individually. Check our Rules page for more details.

How do I report a player or issue?

If you encounter rule violations or have concerns, please report them to our moderators through Discord. Open a support ticket with details and evidence if possible.

Can I appeal a ban?

Yes, you can submit a ban appeal through our Discord server. Please be honest and respectful in your appeal, and our team will review it fairly.

Community Guidelines

Unity SMP is built on respect, creativity, and collaboration. Please follow these guidelines to ensure everyone has a great experience:

Respect & Conduct

  • Treat all community members with kindness and respect
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
  • Keep chat appropriate and family-friendly
  • Respect personal boundaries and privacy

Gameplay Rules

  • No griefing or destroying other players' builds without permission
  • No stealing from other players
  • PvP only with mutual consent
  • No exploiting bugs or using hacked clients
  • Keep the world beautiful - clean up after yourself

Building Guidelines

  • Respect designated community areas and builds
  • Build at least 200 blocks away from other players unless given permission
  • No inappropriate builds or symbols
  • Contribute to community projects when possible

Communication

  • Use Discord for important announcements and discussions
  • Report issues or concerns to moderators privately
  • No spamming in chat or Discord
  • English is the primary language for server chat

Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server, depending on severity. Our moderation team handles each case fairly and individually.
